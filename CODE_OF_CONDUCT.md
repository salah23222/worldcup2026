# Code of Conduct / مدوّنة السلوك

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.

In short: be respectful, welcoming, and constructive. Harassment, discrimination,
and abusive behavior are not tolerated.

نختصرها: كن محترماً ومرحِّباً وبنّاءً؛ لا مكان للتحرّش أو الإساءة.

Violations may be reported confidentially to the maintainers at
`conduct@example.com` *(replace with your address)*. All reports will be reviewed
and handled fairly. By participating, you agree to uphold this code.
